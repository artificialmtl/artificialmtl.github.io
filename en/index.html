What

ARTificial is a space where art and artificial intelligence techniques meet.

When and where

The ARTificial first visual arts exhibit will be held in Montreal at EspacePOP (5587 Park Avenue, Montréal, Qc) on December 6-7 2018.

Call for contributions

Deadline for submissions (first call): November 12th, 2018.

Deadline for submissions (second call): November 20th, 2018.

Second call information:Upcoming...

We are looking for artistic projects which make use of artifical intelligence techniques.

Emphasis will be put on the originality of the project and on the artistic process execution.

We would like to especially encourage submissions from non-professional artists, artists who are building their first project and people without a traditional art education.

Submissions should include the following, in a single .pdf document sent to artificialmtl@gmail.com:

  • Explanation of the artistic process
  • 2 to 3 images, a video or an online demo of each work submitted for consideration
  • A short biography of the artist
