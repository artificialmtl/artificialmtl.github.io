Last update: October 16 2018

What

ARTificial Mtl is a space where art and artificial intelligence techniques meet.

When and where

ARTificial MTL first visual arts exhibit will be held in Montreal at EspacePOP (5587 Park Avenue, Montreal Qc) on December 6-7 2018.

Contributions call

Last day for submissions: November 12th, 2018.

We are looking for artistic projects that uses artifical intelligence techniques.

Emphasis will be on the originality of the proposed project and on the artistic process execution.

We would like to encourage submissions for people who are into their first artistic projects

Submissions should include the following, in a single .pdf document sent to artificialmtl@gmail.com:

  • Explanation of the artistic process
  • 2 to 3 images, a video or an online demo of each work submitted for consideration
  • A short biography of the artist
