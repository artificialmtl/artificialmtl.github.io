When and where

The ARTificial first visual arts exhibit will be held in Montreal at EspacePOP (5587 Park Avenue, Montréal, Qc) on December 6-7 2018.

Hours

Thursday, December 6th 2018: noon (12h00) to nine in the evening (9PM)

Friday, December 7th 2018: noon (12h00) to eight in the evening (8PM)

How to get there

Espace POP is located in the heart of the Mile-End neighborhood, close to the corner of Park Avenue and Saint-Viateur.

Here are suggestions regarding possible ways to get to Espace POP.

From Place-d'Armes station: Take the 80 North bus. The stop is located at the corner of Saint-Laurent and Saint-Antoine.

From Laurier station: Take the 51 bus and stop at Park Avenue. Talk the 80 North bus to Saint-Viateur or walk on Park Avenue towards the North up until Saint-Viateur.
